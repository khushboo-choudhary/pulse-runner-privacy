Privacy Policy

App Name: Pulse Runner
Package: com.pulseapps.pulserunner
Effective Date: May 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Pulse Runner ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile game.

1. Information We Collect

Pulse Runner does not collect, store, or share any personal data with us or any third parties.

The app does not require user registration, login, or account creation.

The following data is stored locally on your device only using Android SharedPreferences:

  • High score
  • Total coins earned
  • Number of games played
  • Selected player skin
  • Unlocked skins
  • Sound and music preferences

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

2. Third-Party Services

The current version of Pulse Runner does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools.

In future versions, we may integrate services such as Google AdMob to display advertisements. These services may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies. When such services are added, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

Learn more: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Children's Privacy

Pulse Runner does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13. The app does not require account creation, login, or any personal information to play.

4. Permissions

The app requests the following Android permissions:

  • WAKE_LOCK – Keeps the screen on during gameplay so the game does not pause unexpectedly.
  • INTERNET – Required for future features or third-party services such as ads. The current version does not actively transmit user data.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

All locally stored data (scores, coins, preferences) can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the app from your device.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
